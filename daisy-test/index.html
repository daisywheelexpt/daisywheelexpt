Daisywheel Input Testing

Please read all the following instructions and then begin the test:

  1. Select the text entry box below.
  2. Move the analog stick to register the controller
  3. Use the Daisywheel input with your controller to type the sentence below.

    the quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

  4. Hit the Escape button on your keyboard when done
  5. Wait for the results to calculate.
  6. Repeat previous steps 5 times in total, producing 5 sets of results.
  7. Hit Save Results below.
  8. Attach the results to an email and send to: whoever@wherever.com
